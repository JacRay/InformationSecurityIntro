1) Principle of Minimizing Secret:
    Security of a system shouldn't be entirely dependent on its implementation not being seen by others(Secrecy)

2) Principle of Complete Mediation:
    Access to system should be checked in all access point

3) Principle of Fail-Safe Defaults:
    If a system fails the defaults should be Safe
    (Example: If a firewall fails it sholud block all traffic other than allowing all traffic)

4) Principle of Least Privilege:
    A person or a process should only have the minimum permission they need to perfrom a task.

5) Principle of Economy of Mechanism:
    Make it simple stupid(Complex function more bugs)

6) Principle of Least Common Mechanism:
    Avoid shared resources to avoid Dos
